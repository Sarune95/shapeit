Hatcha Yoga

Hatcha practices the most basic poses of yoga

ADHO MUKHA SVANASANA- Downward Facing Dog Pose

Instructions to complete the pose as follows:

  1. Lie down on your abdomen.
  2. Keep your legs together and bring your palms closer to your chest.
  3. Lift your chest up by coming down to the Downward Facing Dog Pose.

SALAGHASANA-Locus Pose

Instructions to complete the pose as follows:

  1. Lie down on your stomach on your yoga mat.
  2. Your palms of the hands should be placed along side of your torso, they have to be up and the forehead should rest on the floor.
  3. Breath out and lift your head up, arms and legs also up in the air.
  4. Hold the pose for at least 60 seconds, while releasing the pose slowly breath out.

VRIKSHASANA-The Tree Pose

Instructions to complete the pose as follows:

  1. Strand straight on your mat.
  2. Lift your right leg and place the feet on the left thigh.
  3. Put your hands together in the Namaskar position in front of your chest.
  4. Breath and hold the pose for 60 seconds.
  5. Repeat the steps from the start with your left leg.

TADASANA- The Mountain Pose

Instructions to complete the pose as follows:

  1. Stand on your mat with your feet together, holding your arms at your sides.
  2. Inhale deeply and tuck your tailbone inside.
  3. Hold the pose for at least 60 seconds and then relax.
