Downward Facing Dog

Lie down on your abdomen.

Keep your legs together and bring your palms closer to your chest.

Lift your chest up by coming down to the downward facing dog pose.

Locus Pose

Lie down flat on your abdomen.

Your palms of the hands should be placed beneath your thighs.

Breath out and place your head on your chin, your face must not be on the floor. Place your legs up in the air as high as you can.

Make sure your knees remain straight as your legs are lifted.

Hold the pose for at least 5 seconds, while releasing the pose slowly breath out.

Tree Pose

Stand straight on your mat.

Lift your right leg up and place the feet on the left thigh.

Put your hands together in the namaskar position in front of your chest.

Breath and hold the pose for 60 seconds.

Repeat the steps from the start with your left leg.

Cobra Pose

Lie down on your mat, place your hands flat on the ground directly under your shoulders.

Bend your elbows straight back and hug them into your sides.

Breath and hold the pose for a moment.
