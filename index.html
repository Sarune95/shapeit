What is yoga?

Yoga is a form of exercise which is very popular. Yoga exercise helps to release the tension in our bodys muscles to give us flexibility and to strenghten our muscles. While practising yoga we calming our mind and relaxing our body by concentrating on doing required poses and most of all deep breathing which is hugely important in this form of exercise. There are many types of yoga, down below there is the benefits of yoga and few of most popular types.


The benefits of yoga

  • Reducing stress, anxiety, depression and chronic pain.
  • Promoting better breading.
  • Improves sleep and over all health.
  • Helps you to build muscle strengths while enhancing flexibility.

Types of yoga

  • Hatcha yoga: Hatcha is a name for physical and mental basic poses of yoga which aims to prime the body and mind.
  • Yin yoga: Targets deep tissue. ligaments, joints, bones. Focuses on holding passive poses for long periods of time.
  • Restorative yoga: Promotes complete relaxation while doing the poses.
  • Prenatal yoga: This type of yoga is specifically for moms to be. Helps to ease the pain of the muscles by light exercise poses and the breathing exercise which surely will come in handy for the labor and delivery of the baby.
